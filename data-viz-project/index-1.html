Hispanic Students Underrepresented at UMD's Philip Merrill College of Journalism

Findings from UMD's enrollment report

By Laura Wortman

The University of Maryland's enrollment report shows the drastic decrease in Hispanic students enrolled at the journalism school. Although COVID-19 greatly impacted the decrease in enrollment, the declining trend began as early as Fall 2015. Among the universities smallest enrolled colleges, Philip Merril School of Journalism holds the smallest percentage of Hispanic students.
